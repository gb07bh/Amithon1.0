Why Join Amithon?

Welcome to Amithon 1.0 :
A Hackathon Championship Organized by Google Developer Student Club of Amity University Mumbai.

  • A hackathon is a competition and a social event that brings computer programmers and other interested people together to think of innovative solutions to real world problems and to improve upon or build a new software program.
  • The participants work rapidly to achieve their task, as the events generally only last 24 hours or take place over a weekend.
  • Those participating in a hackathon will work with like-minded individuals to utilize new technologies and hack together tons of solutions from different sources to achieve the goal.

Security & Surveillance

Security related issues are growing. Almost all the situations in present days require a complete security & safety plan. The increased use of automated technologies is driving an increase in the advancement of the need for proper security and surveillance systems. Develop smart solutions to tackle problems faced in the field of security and surveillance.

Sustainable Development Goals

Sustainable development meets the needs of the present without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs. The ultimate goal of sustainable development is to improve health and education, reducing inequality, spurring economic growth, tackling climate change and preserving oceans and forests. Think of ideas that will contribute in meeting the sustainable development goals.

Aid & Growth of Business

Business growth is vital to any company’s success in today’s highly competitive environment, especially as customers become more demanding. Choose correctly, and your growth takes off. In this fast-trending world, many innovative ideas have turned into significant business adventures. Here, we are looking forward to a new business adventure.

Open Innovation

Open innovation is basically an alternative to the conventional method of doing innovation where information has to stay within preset confines. A mindset, if you will, of being open to sharing and receiving information. Build any hack irrespective of any Domain & Sector.

Intelligent Mental Health

Mental health plays important role in achieving global development goals. Many mental health conditions can be effectively treated at relatively low cost, yet the gap between people needing care and those with access to care remains substantial. Effective treatment coverage remains extremely low. Develop unique solutions that will help in improving the Mental HealthCare Sector

Blockchain

Blockchain is one of today's upcoming technologies; it is said to have the potential to change the face of the world. Develop Decentralized applications using our sponsors' platforms (Polygon, Tezos, Celo, Filecoin) and be eligible to win exciting prizes.
